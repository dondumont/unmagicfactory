I bet you think a lot about dying. It's near impossible not to these days with all war, pollution, and duende creeping through the windows to whisper dark nothings from the negative dimension beneath your pillow. Sure, that's all sexy and good but what would it be like to go beyond death? What would it feel like to transmute the dull lump of wilting flesh you are now into a being of pure party energy? A being only hinted at in the Book of Enoch and rumored to be tattooed on the inner thigh of Zosimos. It probably feels exactly like playing Swig N' Sealed. Transcend with us.
